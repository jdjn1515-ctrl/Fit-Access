How It Works

Get your gym up and running in minutes

1

Members Receive Invite

When someone joins your gym, send them an invite code via email or SMS. They download the Fit Access app and link their membership instantly.

2

Secure Setup

Members enable Face ID/Touch ID (optional but recommended) and set up a passkey for password-free login. All in under 2 minutes.

3

QR Code Access

Members open the app, their QR code appears instantly. They scan it at your entry reader and the door unlocks. That's it.

4

Automated Validation

The system checks membership status, access schedule, and token validity in real-time. Entry logs are saved for your records.
